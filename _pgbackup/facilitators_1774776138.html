Facilitators

Each facilitator brings deep practice, embodied wisdom, and a genuine commitment to holding space for transformation. They are not teachers standing above—they are fellow travelers walking alongside.

Ana Silva

Meditation & Silence

Twenty years of Vipassana practice. Ana guides with quiet authority and boundless compassion, creating containers where silence becomes revelation.

João Costa

Nature Connection

Ecologist, forager, and deep listener. João's gift is helping people remember their belonging to the more-than-human world.

Maria Lopes

Creative Practice

Poet and visual artist. Maria believes creativity is not a skill but a way of being—available to everyone willing to show up honestly.

Rafael Santos

Embodiment & Movement

Somatic therapist and dance facilitator. Rafael guides participants back into the body's intelligence through conscious movement and breathwork.

Sofia Mendes

Yoga & Breathwork

Certified in multiple lineages, Sofia teaches yoga not as gymnastics but as a practice of coming home to the present moment.

Tomás Reis

Sound Healing

Sound therapist working with gongs, singing bowls, and voice. Tomás creates sonic landscapes that invite deep rest and cellular healing.

Are you a facilitator?

We're always open to collaborating with practitioners who share our values of presence, integrity, and deep respect for the process of transformation.
