Tenda 21

Shelter for Presence

In the quiet,
we remember who we are.

Tenda 21 is not an escape. It is a return—to presence, to the body, to the earth beneath our feet. Here, time moves differently. Days unfold without the relentless pulse of notifications, the weight of screens, the noise that drowns out our own thoughts.

We offer space. Not empty space, but space filled with birdsong, the smell of rain on stone, the texture of linen, the warmth of shared silence. Space to breathe fully. Space to listen. Space to simply be.

This is where you practice the art of living deliberately—where every moment is an invitation to arrive, again and again, in the only place we ever truly are: here, now.

Immersions

Silent Immersion

Three days of noble silence. Guided meditation, mindful movement, and contemplative practices. Learn to hear the voice beneath the noise.

3–5 Days

Nature Practice

Forest bathing, wild foraging, earth-based rituals. Restore your relationship with the living world. Remember that you are nature, not separate from it.

4–7 Days

Creative Retreat

Write, draw, make. Creativity flows when the mind quiets. Supported by gentle structure, ample solitude, and evening gatherings around the fire.

5–10 Days

The Space

Details

Location

Nestled in the foothills of Serra da Estrela, Portugal. Two hours from Porto, a world away from everywhere else.

Accommodation

Private canvas tents with wooden floors, linen bedding, and wood-burning stoves. Shared bathhouse with hot water and composting toilets.

What's Included

Three seasonal vegetarian meals daily, guided practices, access to trails and natural swimming spots, herbal tea library, journal and pen.

Not Included

Your phone. (We lock them away on arrival. You'll thank us later.)

we're here.
